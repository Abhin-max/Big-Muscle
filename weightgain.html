Weekly Workout for Weight gain

Monday: Upper Body Strength Training

  • Bench Press: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Bent-Over Rows: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Overhead Dumbbell Press: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Pull-Ups: 3 sets to failure

Focus on compound movements to maximize muscle gain.

Tuesday: Lower Body Strength Training

  • Squats: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Deadlifts: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 8-10 reps per leg
  • Calf Raises: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Ensure proper form to prevent injuries and increase weight gradually.

Wednesday: Rest Day or Active Recovery

Light stretching or yoga for flexibility. Focus on hydration and nutrition to support muscle recovery.

Thursday: Full Body Workout

  • Deadlifts: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Push-Ups: 4 sets to failure
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds

Engage all major muscle groups to stimulate growth.

Friday: High-Calorie Cardio

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) for 20-30 minutes. Keep the intensity high to stimulate appetite without burning too many calories.

Saturday: Core and Flexibility Training

  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Bicycle Crunches: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Leg Raises: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Stretching: 15-20 minutes

A strong core supports overall strength gains.

Sunday: Meal Prep and Rest

Plan and prepare high-calorie meals for the week. Ensure you have enough protein and calories to support muscle growth.
