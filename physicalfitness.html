Weekly Workout for Physical Fitness

Monday: Base Run

Workout 1: 3-5 miles at a comfortable pace.

Workout 2: Maintain a steady heart rate.

Workout 3: 5-10 minutes cool down (walking or light jog).

Tuesday: Speed Work

  • Workout 1: Warm-up: 10 minutes easy jog.
  • Workout 2: 400m sprints, 400m jog (4-6 reps).
  • Workout 3: Cool down: 10 minutes easy jog.

Wednesday: Rest or Cross-Training

Workout 1: 20-30 minutes light cycling or swimming.

Workout 2: Stretching: 15 minutes (focus on legs).

Workout 3: Yoga or Pilates: 20 minutes recovery.

Thursday: Tempo Run

Workout 1: Warm-up: 10 minutes easy jog.

Workout 2: Tempo run: 4-6 miles at a challenging pace.

Workout 3: 5-10 minutes cool down.

Friday: Long Run

Workout 1: 6-10 miles at a comfortable pace.

Workout 2: Gradually increase pace during the run.

Workout 3: Hydrate every 3-4 miles.

Saturday: Hill Repeats

Workout 1: 6-8 hill sprints (30-60 seconds uphill).

Workout 2: Jog or walk down for recovery.

Workout 3: Cool down: 5-10 minutes of easy jog.

Sunday: Recovery Run

Workout 1: 2-3 miles at an easy pace.

Workout 2: 4-6 short strides (20 seconds) during the run.

Workout 3: Stretching: 5-10 minutes to relax muscles.
