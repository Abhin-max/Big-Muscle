Nutrition Plan For Fat Loss

Monday

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and tomatoes, whole grain toast.
  • Snack: 1 medium apple with almond butter.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken breast with mixed greens and balsamic vinaigrette.
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with berries.
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with broccoli and quinoa.

Tuesday

  • Breakfast: Smoothie with spinach, banana, protein powder, and almond milk.
  • Snack: Baby carrots with hummus.
  • Lunch: Turkey lettuce wraps with avocado and diced tomatoes.
  • Snack: 1 hard-boiled egg.
  • Dinner: Stir-fried tofu with bell peppers and cauliflower rice.

Wednesday

  • Breakfast: Overnight oats with almond milk and berries.
  • Snack: A handful of mixed nuts.
  • Lunch: Quinoa salad with black beans and corn.
  • Snack: Celery sticks with peanut butter.
  • Dinner: Grilled shrimp with asparagus and wild rice.

Thursday

  • Breakfast: Chia pudding with almond milk and banana.
  • Snack: 1 small orange.
  • Lunch: Lentil soup with a side salad.
  • Snack: Cottage cheese with pineapple.
  • Dinner: Baked chicken thighs with Brussels sprouts and sweet potato.

Friday

  • Breakfast: Whole grain toast with avocado and poached egg.
  • Snack: Sliced bell peppers with guacamole.
  • Lunch: Tuna salad on a bed of greens.
  • Snack: 1 small apple or pear.
  • Dinner: Beef stir-fry with mixed vegetables over brown rice.

Saturday

  • Breakfast: Protein pancakes topped with maple syrup.
  • Snack: 1 cup of strawberries or blueberries.
  • Lunch: Spinach salad with grilled chicken and walnuts.
  • Snack: 1 hard-boiled egg.
  • Dinner: Zucchini noodles with marinara sauce and turkey meatballs.

Sunday

  • Breakfast: Smoothie with kale, banana, and protein powder.
  • Snack: A handful of almonds.
  • Lunch: Chickpea salad with cucumber and tomatoes.
  • Snack: Sliced cucumber with tzatziki sauce.
  • Dinner: Roast chicken with roasted vegetables.
