Nutrition Plan For Weight Gain

Monday

  • Breakfast: 3 scrambled eggs with spinach and feta cheese, whole grain toast.
  • Snack: 1 banana with peanut butter.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken breast with quinoa and broccoli.
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with mixed berries and granola.
  • Dinner: Salmon fillet with brown rice and asparagus.

Tuesday

  • Breakfast: Protein smoothie with whey protein, spinach, banana, and almond milk.
  • Snack: 1 apple with mixed nuts.
  • Lunch: Turkey burger with sweet potato fries and a side salad.
  • Snack: Cottage cheese with pineapple.
  • Dinner: Beef stir-fry with bell peppers and brown rice.

Wednesday

  • Breakfast: Overnight oats with oats, almond milk, almonds, and banana.
  • Snack: Hard-boiled eggs.
  • Lunch: Lentil soup with whole grain bread and salad.
  • Snack: Hummus with carrot and cucumber sticks.
  • Dinner: Grilled shrimp tacos with corn tortillas and avocado.

Thursday

  • Breakfast: Chia pudding with chia seeds, almond milk, and berries.
  • Snack: 1 small orange with walnuts.
  • Lunch: Quinoa bowl with black beans, corn, diced tomatoes, and chicken.
  • Snack: Protein bar or protein balls.
  • Dinner: Baked chicken thighs with roasted sweet potatoes and green beans.

Friday

  • Breakfast: French toast with Greek yogurt and fresh fruit.
  • Snack: Smoothie with protein powder and berries.
  • Lunch: Tuna salad with greens and chickpeas.
  • Snack: Rice cakes with almond butter and banana.
  • Dinner: Pork tenderloin with brown rice and steamed broccoli.

Saturday

  • Breakfast: Omelet with eggs, bell peppers, onions, and cheese.
  • Snack: Trail mix (nuts, seeds, dried fruit).
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken Caesar salad.
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with honey and walnuts.
  • Dinner: Pasta with ground turkey meat sauce and mixed vegetables.

Sunday

  • Breakfast: Smoothie bowl with protein powder, banana, granola, and fruit.
  • Snack: Sliced cucumber and bell peppers with tzatziki sauce.
  • Lunch: Chickpea salad with cucumber, tomato, and feta.
  • Snack: Protein shake.
  • Dinner: Roast beef with mashed potatoes and steamed carrots.
