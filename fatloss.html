Weekly Workout for Fat Loss

Monday: HIIT Cardio

  • 20 seconds sprint, 40 seconds walk (repeat for 20-30 minutes).

Push intensity during sprints.

Tuesday: Strength Training (Full Body)

  • Squats: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Push-Ups: 3 sets to failure
  • Dumbbell Deadlifts: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds

Focus on higher reps to increase calorie burn.

Wednesday: Steady-State Cardio

Jogging, cycling, or swimming for 30-45 minutes.

Maintain a moderate intensity.

Thursday: Core and Abs Focus

  • Bicycle Crunches: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Leg Raises: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Plank Variations: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds
  • Side Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds each side

Engage your core throughout.

Friday: Full Body Circuit

  • Jump Squats: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Burpees: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Kettlebell Swings: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Push-Ups: 3 sets to failure

Minimize rest between exercises.

Saturday: Active Recovery

Yoga or light stretching.

Focus on muscle recovery.

Sunday: Meal Prep and Planning

Prepare healthy meals for the week. Include plenty of lean proteins and vegetables.
