Nutrition Plan For Overall health

Monday

  • Breakfast: Greek yogurt with mixed berries and granola.
  • Snack: 1 medium apple with almond butter.
  • Lunch: Quinoa salad with chickpeas, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, and lemon-tahini dressing.
  • Snack: Carrot sticks with hummus.
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with steamed broccoli and sweet potato.

Tuesday

  • Breakfast: Overnight oats with almond milk, chia seeds, and banana.
  • Snack: A handful of mixed nuts.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken wrap with whole grain tortilla, spinach, avocado, and salsa.
  • Snack: Celery sticks with cottage cheese.
  • Dinner: Stir-fried tofu with mixed vegetables and brown rice.

Wednesday

  • Breakfast: Smoothie with spinach, banana, protein powder, and almond milk.
  • Snack: 1 orange or a small bowl of berries.
  • Lunch: Lentil soup with a side salad.
  • Snack: Sliced bell peppers with guacamole.
  • Dinner: Grilled shrimp with quinoa and roasted Brussels sprouts.

Thursday

  • Breakfast: Whole grain toast with avocado and poached egg.
  • Snack: Hard-boiled egg and a piece of fruit.
  • Lunch: Spinach salad with grilled chicken, walnuts, and feta cheese.
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with honey.
  • Dinner: Turkey chili with kidney beans and tomatoes.

Friday

  • Breakfast: Chia seed pudding with almond milk and mixed fruits.
  • Snack: Rice cakes with almond butter.
  • Lunch: Brown rice bowl with black beans, corn, and diced tomatoes.
  • Snack: Cucumber slices with tzatziki sauce.
  • Dinner: Baked chicken with roasted vegetables.

Saturday

  • Breakfast: Smoothie bowl with mixed fruits, granola, and seeds.
  • Snack: Trail mix (nuts and dried fruit).
  • Lunch: Whole grain pasta with marinara sauce and a side salad.
  • Snack: Baby carrots with hummus.
  • Dinner: Grilled fish tacos with cabbage slaw and avocado.

Sunday

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal topped with sliced almonds and berries.
  • Snack: Sliced pear with cheese.
  • Lunch: Quinoa salad with roasted vegetables and feta cheese.
  • Snack: Edamame.
  • Dinner: Roast chicken with sweet potato and steamed green beans.
