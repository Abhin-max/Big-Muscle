Weekly Workout for Strength Training

Monday: Upper Body Strength

  • Bench Press: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Bent-Over Rows: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Overhead Dumbbell Press: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Pull-Ups: 3 sets to failure

Focus on form to avoid injuries.

Tuesday: Lower Body Strength

  • Squats: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Deadlifts: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Leg Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Calf Raises: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Engage your core during lifts.

Wednesday: Core and Stability Training

  • Plank: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Hanging Leg Raises: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Stability Ball Pass: 3 sets of 10 reps

Focus on controlled movements.

Thursday: Full Body Strength

  • Deadlifts: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Push-Ups: 4 sets to failure
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg

Alternate muscle groups for recovery.

Friday: Active Recovery

Light stretching, yoga, or walking.
Prioritize hydration .
nutrition.

Saturday: Strength Endurance

  • Circuit training (2-3 rounds):
  • Push-Ups: 10-15 reps
  • Bodyweight Squats: 15-20 reps
  • Mountain Climbers: 30 seconds
  • Dumbbell Rows: 10-12 reps each arm

Keep rest periods short.

Sunday: Rest Day

Rest and recovery; focus on meal prep.
Ensure proper nutrition for muscle repair.
