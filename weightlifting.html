Weekly Workout for Weight Lifting

Monday: Push Day (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)

  • Bench Press: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Overhead Press: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Dips: 3 sets to failure
  • Tricep Extensions: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Warm-up before lifting heavy.

Tuesday: Pull Day (Back, Biceps)

  • Pull-Ups: 4 sets to failure
  • Bent-Over Rows: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Barbell Curls: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Face Pulls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Focus on a full range of motion.

Wednesday: Leg Day

  • Squats: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Deadlifts: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Leg Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Calf Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Engage your core throughout.

Thursday: Accessory Day

  • Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Flyes: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds

Work on weak areas or muscle imbalances.

Friday: Heavy Lifting Day

  • Bench Press: 4 sets of 4-6 reps
  • Deadlifts: 4 sets of 4-6 reps
  • Squats: 4 sets of 4-6 reps

Focus on increasing weights gradually.

Saturday: Active Recovery or Light Cardio

Light cardio or yoga. Prioritize recovery and hydration.

Sunday: Rest Day

Rest and meal prep. Ensure adequate nutrition for recovery.
