Weekly Workout for Running

Monday: Base Run

Distance: 3-5 miles at a comfortable pace.

Focus on maintaining a steady heart rate.

Tuesday: Speed Work

  • Warm-up: 10 minutes easy jog.
  • Intervals: 400m sprints followed by 400m jogs (repeat 4-6 times).
  • Cool down: 10 minutes easy jog.

Aim to improve pace with each interval.

Wednesday: Rest Day or Cross-Training

Light cycling, swimming, or yoga. Focus on flexibility and recovery.

Thursday: Tempo Run

Distance: 4-6 miles. Start easy, then increase to a challenging pace for the middle miles, cool down at the end.

Build endurance with sustained effort.

Friday: Long Run

Distance: 6-10 miles at a comfortable pace.

Hydrate well and consider fuel for longer runs.

Saturday: Hill Repeats

Find a hill and do 6-8 repeats (run uphill at a hard effort, jog or walk back down).

Focus on form when running uphill.

Sunday: Recovery Run

Distance: 2-3 miles at an easy pace. Use this run to loosen up after the week’s training.
